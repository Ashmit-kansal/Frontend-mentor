Paneer Bhurji

A quick, flavorful North Indian dish made with crumbled paneer (Indian cottage cheese), spices, and vegetables. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner with roti or paratha.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Optional vegetables: capsicum, peas

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Crumble the paneer using your hands or a fork. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil: In a pan, heat oil or ghee over medium flame. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Sauté onions: Add chopped onions and green chili. Sauté until the onions are golden brown.
  4. Cook tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  5. Add spices: Mix in turmeric and red chili powder. Cook for a few seconds.
  6. Add paneer: Add crumbled paneer, salt, and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Garnish and serve: Turn off the heat, garnish with chopped coriander leaves, and serve hot.
